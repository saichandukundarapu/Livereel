FILIM PREVISUALIZATION

Film Previsualization

Film Previsualization (Previs) is the process of planning and visualizing scenes using rough 3D layouts, camera movements, and timing before actual filming starts. At Livereel Visual Factory, we create detailed previs to help directors and production teams understand shot composition, action flow, and visual effects requirements in advance. This reduces production risks, saves time and costs, and ensures a clear creative vision for films, commercials, and digital projects.

OUR SERVCIES

Story & Concept Planning

Visualizing scenes early to define story flow, pacing, and creative direction.

Camera & Shot Desig

Planning camera angles, movements, and framing before the shoot begins.

VFX & Technical Planning

Mapping visual effects, CGI integration, and technical requirements in advance

Time & Cost Optimization

Reducing reshoots and production risks by finalizing scenes before filming.
