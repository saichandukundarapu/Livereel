Visualisation Supervisors

Visualisation Supervisors play a key role in shaping the creative and technical vision of a project. They oversee the planning, execution, and integration of visual elements across previs, VFX, and final delivery. At Livereel Visual Factory, our Visualisation Supervisors collaborate closely with directors, VFX teams, and production units to ensure visual consistency, accurate storytelling, and seamless translation from concept to screen. Their expertise ensures that creative intent, technical precision, and production efficiency are aligned throughout the project lifecycle..
