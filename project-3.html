Our Journey in Tollywood

Livereel Visual Factory has contributed VFX and post-production support to several notable Tollywood films, including Akhil, Maharshi, Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy, Baahubali, Kamma Rajyam Lo Kadapa Reddlu, Playback, Sardar Gabbar Singh, Jakkanna, and Tikka. Working across diverse genres and production scales, our team delivered reliable visual effects support aligned with industry standards and cinematic storytelling requirements.

Tollywood

Through these Tollywood projects, Livereel Visual Factory gained valuable experience working within high-pressure production schedules and large-scale film pipelines. Our team collaborated closely with directors, production houses, and senior VFX supervisors to ensure every shot met creative and technical expectations. This hands-on industry exposure continues to shape our production quality and training methodology, allowing us to deliver consistent results across all projects.
